Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 19, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Nickname, age, gender, and other details you provide during registration
  • Questionnaire Responses: Your answers to our 66-question values assessment questionnaire
  • Profile Information: Optional details you choose to share in your profile
  • Chat Messages: Content of messages exchanged with other users
  • Usage Data: How you use the app, including matches, ratings, and interactions
  • Device Information: IP address, device type, operating system, and unique identifiers

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our services
  • Match you with like-minded people using our AI algorithm
  • Facilitate communication between users
  • Process payments and purchases
  • Send you notifications and updates
  • Prevent fraud and ensure app security
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Information Sharing & Third Parties

We respect your privacy and never share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information may be shared:

  • With Other Users: Only with mutual approval through our anonymous matching system. Your profile is fully anonymous until both parties are satisfied with each other
  • Service Providers: With trusted third parties who assist in operating our app (e.g., cloud hosting, payment processing)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Third-Party AI Data Sharing Policy

VitaDuo uses a self-hosted AI system. Your assessment responses, chat messages, and personal data are never shared with any third-party AI service providers (such as OpenAI, Google AI, etc.). All AI computation is performed on our own servers, ensuring your data always remains under our control.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest (TLS 1.3 and AES-256)
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Anonymization of sensitive personal data
  • Encrypted storage of server-side data

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data (account deletion is available directly in the app)
  • Export: Receive all your data in a structured format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data
  • Restriction: Request restriction of data processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at ab@tpr.wales

6. Account Deletion & Data Export

You can delete your account at any time directly within the app. When you delete your account:

  • All personal information (nickname, age, gender, questionnaire responses, etc.) is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Chat messages may be retained for the other party involved, but your identity information will be anonymized
  • Anonymized usage statistics may be retained for service improvement
  • You can request a full export of your personal data via ab@tpr.wales before deletion

7. Anonymous Matching System

VitaDuo's unique anonymous matching system ensures:

  • Users are identified by random codes (e.g., U-K8X3M) until mutual approval
  • Personal information (name, photo, contact) remains hidden
  • Only after both users are satisfied with each other are profiles unlocked
  • You control when and with whom you share your real identity

8. Cookie Policy

The VitaDuo website and app use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Essential Cookies: Maintain login state and basic app functionality
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences (e.g., language selection)
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymously collect usage data to improve our service

You can manage your cookie preferences through your device settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect app functionality.

9. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to provide our services. Specific retention policies:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for the duration of the account
  • Deleted Accounts: All personal information is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Chat Messages: May be retained for the other party involved, but sender information is anonymized
  • Anonymized Statistics: Anonymized usage data may be retained for analysis and service improvement

10. Children's Privacy

VitaDuo is intended for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will delete it immediately.

11. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Emailing you before significant changes take effect
  • Posting the new policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

13. Contact Us

Website: https://dd3.tpr.wales

For privacy concerns specific to your region, please include your location in your inquiry.

This Privacy Policy is designed to comply with GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation), CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act), and other applicable privacy regulations.
